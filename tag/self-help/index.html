Influence, The psychology of persuasion – Robert B Cialdini (10/10)

October 6, 2018 by Rahul S Rajan Leave a Comment

One of the most influential books I have read on psychology. The book details all the methods used by marketers to capture our attention and to persuade our actions. This can be something as small as laughter tracks in comedy sitcoms or more profound like influencing the actions of POWs in Chinese prison camps
